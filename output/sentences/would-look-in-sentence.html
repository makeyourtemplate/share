Using 'would look' in a sentence

'would look' in a Sentence

  • I like the motif. It would look fine in my living room.
  • Maybe a rougher feeling would look better.

'would look' in an Interrogative Sentence

  • I wonder what the wedding dress you'd make me would look like?
  • So what kind of new hairstyle do you think would look good on her?

'would look' in a Suggested Sentence

  • It would be best if I had my personal physician look at me.
  • Sir Warrior would come to look for me.
