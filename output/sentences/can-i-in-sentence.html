Using 'can i' in a sentence

'can i' in a Sentence

  • can i have your attention? The redeye to New York is about to board.
  • can i borrow it, please? I love him.

'can i' in an Interrogative Sentence

  • Tell me, what can i do with this little girl?
  • can i wrap this up already?

'can i' in a Suggested Sentence

  • That is all i can do now.
  • i believe he can definitely defeat Sir Warrior and his companions.
