Using 'bother me' in a sentence

'bother me' in a Sentence

  • Don't bother me with your stupid questions.
  • Physical pain doesn't bother me that much.

'bother me' in an Interrogative Sentence

  • Have you come to bother me?
  • Now, listen, Sugar. Today I don't want you to bother me. All right?

'bother me' in a Suggested Sentence

  • But it's such a bother to me as you study for this purpose.
  • Don't bother Buddha with my problems. He might just lock me up again.
