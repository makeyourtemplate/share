Using 'now know' in a sentence

'now know' in a Sentence

  • I now know the reason why.
  • I guess we now know who the real man of this house is.

'now know' in an Interrogative Sentence

  • And do you now know that he cannot be reasoned with?
  • You now know it is Victor. When you write with invisible ink, right?

'now know' in a Suggested Sentence

  • I was sick of this world, but now I know it isn't so bad.
  • now you know what I am capable of.
