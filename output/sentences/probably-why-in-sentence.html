Using 'probably why' in a sentence

'probably why' in a Sentence

  • probably why you're not answering the phone.

'probably why' in an Interrogative Sentence

  • That's probably why she's so fat, huh?
  • It's probably why I found poker, huh?

'probably why' in a Suggested Sentence

  • It's probably starving, that's why it's eating whatever it finds.
  • Dude, why don't we just take our ship? It's probably faster.
